## What 6sense is

6sense is the AI-powered ABM platform for B2B revenue teams — marketing,
sales, and revenue operations — built around a single thesis:

**Most of the buying journey happens before a prospect ever fills out a form.**
6sense surfaces that anonymous research activity, identifies which accounts
are actually in-market, and lets revenue teams act on those signals across
every channel — automatically.

Public positioning: *"The ABM Platform Powered by Revenue Intelligence"* /
*"AI That Knows What Matters. Agents That Act on It."*

## The product, in plain language

6sense unifies three things most companies cobble together from point tools:

1. **Signalverse — the data layer.** Captures ~1 trillion signals per day
   across intent, company firmographic, and contact data. Knows when accounts
   are researching specific topics on or off the customer's website.
2. **6AI — the intelligence layer.** Predictive scoring, in-market account
   identification, buying-stage prediction, recommended next actions.
3. **Execution surfaces — where teams act on the signals.**
   - **Revenue Marketing**: AI Email Agents, digital advertising, intelligent
     workflows, audience builder, smart form fill, reporting.
   - **Sales Intelligence**: Sales Copilot, account prioritization, list
     builder, sales alerts, company & people search.
   - **Predictive Analytics**: scoring models for marketing and sales hand-off.

A single drag-and-drop canvas orchestrates ads, email, web, and sales
together — instead of running each channel in its own silo.
